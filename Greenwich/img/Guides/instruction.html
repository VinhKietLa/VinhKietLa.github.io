Bolt on upgrades

  • Full Induction kit
  • Exhaust system:Headers,Cat-back and axleback
  • Suspension: Coil overs or lowering springs
  • Light-weight alloy wheels
  • Performance tyres

Turbo Upgrades

  • Manifold, downpipe
  • Turbo fitting kit
  • ME221 ECU
  • Intercooler setup
  • Boost control/mapping

What to upgrade?

When deciding on what upgrades to do there are two options, bolt on upgrades that 'bolt' straight on to the vehicle without requiring modifying the existing components whilst still acheiving performance upgrades. Or high performance modifications that will include altering the cars internals (turbo/supercharging)
