History

The Royal Borough of Greenwich is a London borough in south-east London ,England. Taking its name from the historic town of Greenwich, the London Borough of Greenwich was formed in 1965 by the amalgamation of the former area of the Metropolitan Borough of Greenwich with part of the Metropolitan Borough of Woolwich to the east. Greenwich is world-famous as the traditional location of the Prime Meridian, on which all Coordinated Universal Time is based.
